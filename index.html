About Us

Velmont Energy Limited is a diversified natural resources company focused on energy, mining, and mineral wealth creation across Africa. We are committed to responsible exploration and development of oil, gas, and precious stones, including gold, diamonds, lithium, and rare earth minerals.

Our mission is to power economic growth through ethical operations, cutting-edge technology, and strategic partnerships, delivering world-class solutions across the energy value chain.

Our Mission

To unlock natural wealth — from energy to minerals — through innovation, integrity, and sustainable development. We empower local communities, drive industrial transformation, and provide world-class solutions across oil, gas, and solid minerals.

Services

Mining & Precious Minerals Division We explore, mine, process, and market high-value mineral resources: Gold & Diamond Mining – Exploration and extraction of gold and high-grade diamonds in secure zones. Lithium & Rare Earths – Prospecting and mining of lithium, cobalt, and other strategic minerals vital for renewable energy and tech industries. Solid Mineral Trading – Legal, certified buying and global export of precious and industrial stones. Environmental Stewardship – Eco-conscious mining with land rehabilitation and community welfare programs. Our operations are fully licensed under Nigeria’s Mining Act and other global standards.
